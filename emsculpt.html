Frequently Asked Questions

Does it hurt?

No, Emsculpt NEO is not painful. You'll feel intense muscle contractions that take some getting used to, but most people find it tolerable and even enjoyable. The sensation is similar to an intense workout.

How soon will I see results?

You may start feeling muscle engagement immediately after your first session. Visible changes typically begin around 3 weeks, with optimal results appearing at 3 months as your body continues to eliminate fat cells and build muscle.

How many treatments do I need?

The standard protocol is 4 sessions scheduled 5-10 days apart. For rehabilitation purposes, protocols may vary based on your specific condition and recovery goals.

Can I use Emsculpt NEO if I'm overweight?

Emsculpt NEO works best on individuals with a BMI under 35. However, every body is different - schedule a consultation so we can assess if you're a good candidate.

Is there any downtime?

None. You can return to normal activities immediately after treatment. Some people experience muscle soreness similar to an intense workout, which typically resolves within 24-48 hours.

How is this different from other body contouring treatments?

Emsculpt NEO is the only device that simultaneously builds muscle AND reduces fat. Other treatments (like CoolSculpting) only address fat, or (like traditional Emsculpt) only build muscle. Plus, we're the only practice using it for rehabilitation protocols.
